A few words about me

As an automotive professional, I have gathered in my 15+ years of professional life various experiences. Starting as a technician in a Fiat / Alfa Romeo / Maserati workshop in my hometown Bucharest, I have gathered much hands-on experience in servicing cars. This experience would later help me better understand the workflows and processes within a workshop. I applied this experience while taking various roles within the company, from warranty consultant to service advisor. In my role as a service advisor, I had the privilege of interacting with various customers (normal customers, fleet customers, international customers, etc.). Discussing with customers, finding solutions to their problems, managing their expectations and the demands of the workshop, I was able to improve dramatically some of my skills: time management, problem solving, and customer relations.

Being thirsty of new challenges and willing to further develop myself, I managed to get a job with Renault Service Roumanie, a sister company for Renault France. I started there as a warranty analyst for Switzerland and climbed up the ladder becoming after a short time Team Manager for the German-speaking countries, Italy and Spain. Interacting with my colleagues from other countries helped me refine my management skills.

The move to Abu Dhabi Motors came as a normal step in my desire to have an international career. I joined the famous Abu Dhabi Motors (biggest BMW, MINI and Rolls Royce dealer at that time) as a team leader in the workshop. It was back to the tools but an experience I will never forget. Having to manage more than 25 people of various cultures and backgrounds has helped me improve further my people management skills, and my time management skills, as well as getting used to working in a very fast-paced environment. The 2.5 years working there played a major role in my development both professionally as personally.

Coming back to Europe, joining Renault Germany as a warranty team leader allowed me to put into practice all my knowledge gathered from previous experiences. During this time I further developed my skills in areas such as customer relations, and budget follow-up. My data analysis skills, my desire to improve processes and procedures and my constant focus on customer satisfaction allowed me to decrease drastically the time needed to answer to dealer requests.

Keeping up with the automotive trend, I joined the electric hype and ventured into the world of start-ups. First joining Vinfast Europe and later Fisker Europe, I was able to learn new things and further develop myself. My experience as a technician from the beginning of my professional life helped me set-up the service network for both start-ups. Putting to good use my time management skills, my technical skills as well as having a ferocious desire to perform and do things as good as possible, allowed me to finish all assigned projects within the allocated timeline and budget.

I am passionate about learning new things and applying my know-how to solve complex challenges and deliver innovative solutions. I have leveraged my skills and certifications in Microsoft Power Automate and LinkedIn Learning to streamline workflows, automate tasks, and enhance my professional development.
